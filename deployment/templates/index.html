Model Description

The model at the backend is based on the Monotonic Attention mechanism in which the attention is focused in a specific diretion (left to right in english). This is different from the normal attention mechanism where all the words are inspected at every time step and weighted sum is done to compute the context vector, but here the inspection starts from the token where the previous time step left of the inspection.
